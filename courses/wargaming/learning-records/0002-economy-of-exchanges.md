# 0002 — The economy of exchanges (L2)

**Date:** 2026-07-06
**Status:** current

## What L2 teaches

Principle 2: every action is a purchase; the only question is the exchange rate = score-difference gained per resource spent. Built directly on L1's measuring stick (value = movement of the score-difference). Resources include models, points, CP, activations, board space, and above all **time** (fixed rounds, never refunded).

Two mispricings the lesson targets:
- **The corpse count** — a unit that dies can still win its trade (its death is the price, not the outcome).
- **Overpaying** — a flawless, no-losses kill can still be a bad trade once **opportunity cost** is counted (the best thing those resources could have done instead).

Discipline: pay the minimum that gets the effect, guard the surplus. Heuristic "sledgehammer to kill an ant" = the *ceiling* on force a task deserves.
