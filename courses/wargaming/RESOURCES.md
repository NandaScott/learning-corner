# Resources — Wargaming Fundamentals

Trusted sources grounding this course. Sun Tzu quotes are verified verbatim; 40K mechanics are verified against the current ruleset. Never teach from parametric memory.

## Primary — the framing (Sun Tzu)

- **[*The Art of War* — Lionel Giles translation (Project Gutenberg #132)](https://www.gutenberg.org/files/132/132-h/132-h.htm)** — the canonical public-domain English translation. Ground truth for every Art-of-War callout. Chapter map used in this course:
  - Ch I — *Laying Plans* — "all warfare is based on deception."
  - Ch II — *Waging War* — the cost of war; feed off the enemy (Principle 2).
  - Ch III — *Attack by Stratagem* — "supreme excellence… breaking the enemy's resistance without fighting"; the force-ratio rules, surround-with-ten / attack-with-five (Principles 1, 4).
  - Ch IV — *Tactical Dispositions* — "the victorious strategist only seeks battle after the victory has been won" (Principle 1).
  - Ch V — *Energy* — stored potential released at the instant; the crossbow, the trigger, the poised stone (Principle 5).
  - Ch VI — *Weak Points and Strong* — impose your will, appear where they must defend, occupy the field first; water has no constant shape (Principle 3).
- **[*The Art of War* — Giles, illustrated Gutenberg edition #17405](https://www.gutenberg.org/files/17405/17405-h/17405-h.htm)** — alternate Gutenberg copy of the same translation; useful cross-check.

## Primary — the instantiation (Warhammer 40,000, 11th edition)

11th edition launched **20 June 2026**. It removed objective *markers* (you now fight over **terrain objectives**, controlled by total **Objective Control**), and the **primary mission is set by the players' force dispositions**. The course is grounded on 11th ed — verify against these, not memory or 10th-ed habits.

- **[Warhammer 40,000 11th Edition Core Rules — official free download (Warhammer Community)](https://www.warhammer-community.com/en-gb/downloads/warhammer-40000/)** — the authoritative primary source: battle rounds, phases, movement, shooting, charges, Objective Control. Ground truth where anything disagrees.
- **[Wahapedia — 40K 11th Edition Core Rules](https://wahapedia.ru/wh40k11ed/the-rules/core-rules/)** — free, searchable mirror/reference for the 11th-ed ruleset. Convenient for lookups; defer to the official PDF on any conflict.
- **11th-edition mission rules** — for how primary/secondary VP are actually earned (force dispositions → primary mission, scoring timing/amounts). **Verify exact VP numbers here before quoting them hard** — the old 50/40 (or 45/45) caps were *10th/9th edition* and do not carry over.

## Secondary — strategy commentary (wisdom, not ground truth)

- **Competitive 40K communities** for testing the theory against real practitioners: the [r/WarhammerCompetitive](https://www.reddit.com/r/WarhammerCompetitive/) subreddit (tactics discussion, "Fireside" weekly threads), and **Goonhammer** (goonhammer.com) for in-depth tactical/meta articles. Use for *wisdom* — how principles play out against skilled opponents — not for rules ground truth.
