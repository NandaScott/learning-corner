## Primary — the framing (Sun Tzu)

- **[*The Art of War* — Lionel Giles translation (Project Gutenberg #132)](https://www.gutenberg.org/files/132/132-h/132-h.htm)** — canonical public-domain English translation; ground truth for every Art-of-War callout.
- **[*The Art of War* — Giles, MIT Internet Classics Archive](https://classics.mit.edu/Tzu/artwar.html)** — searchable mirror of the same translation; convenient cross-check. Chapter map used in this course (advanced-layer picks):
  - Ch I — *Laying Plans* — "all warfare is based on deception"; "Attack him where he is unprepared, appear where you are not expected." → deception & threat overload (L3).
  - Ch V — *Energy* — "not more than two methods of attack — the direct and the indirect; yet these two in combination give rise to an endless series of maneuvers." → the hammer and anvil, *zheng*/*qi* (L4).
  - Ch VI — *Weak Points and Strong* — "The clever combatant imposes his will on the enemy…"; "Appear at points which the enemy must hasten to defend…"; "Whoever is first in the field…will be fresh…second…will arrive exhausted." → tempo & initiative (L1), deployment (L5).

## Primary — the instantiation (Warhammer 40,000, 11th edition)

11th edition launched **20 June 2026**. Grounded on 11th ed — verify against these, not memory or 10th-ed habits.

- **[Warhammer 40,000 11th Edition Core Rules — official free download (Warhammer Community)](https://www.warhammer-community.com/en-gb/downloads/warhammer-40000/)** — authoritative primary source: battle rounds, the seven-part turn, phases, the alternating Fight phase, Objective Control. Ground truth on any conflict.
- **[Wahapedia — 40K 11th Edition Core Rules](https://wahapedia.ru/wh40k11ed/the-rules/core-rules/)** — free searchable mirror. Confirmed facts used here: the game is **I-go-you-go** ("the same player always takes the first turn… once that player's turn has ended, their opponent takes their turn"); a turn is Command → Movement → Shooting → Charge → Fight; **the Fight phase alternates unit selection** (players alternate selecting an eligible unit; Fights First units picked first); objective **control** = higher total OC of models in range, re-checked at end of each phase and turn, and once *secured* stays controlled until the opponent's level exceeds it.
- **11th-edition mission rules** — for how primary/secondary VP are actually earned (force dispositions → primary mission). Verify exact VP numbers here before quoting; do not carry over 10th-ed caps.
