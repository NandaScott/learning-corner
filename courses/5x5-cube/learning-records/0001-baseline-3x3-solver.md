# Baseline: comfortable 3x3 solver, owns a 5x5

Learner solves a 3x3 comfortably and unaided, and has a 5x5 in hand. Mission is a reliable (not fast) unaided 5x5 solve.

**Implications:** Skip all 3x3 content — the reduction endgame is already known. Teach only centers, edge pairing, parity, and how reduction hands off to the 3x3. Every lesson can assign real-cube drills immediately.
