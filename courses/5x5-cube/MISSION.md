# Mission: Solve the 5x5 Cube

## Why
There is a real 5x5 cube in hand, currently unsolved. The goal is a reliable, unaided solve — pick it up scrambled, put it down solved, any time. Speed is explicitly not the goal.

## Success looks like
- Complete a full 5x5 solve from any scramble without notes or videos.
- Recognise and fix the edge-parity case without looking anything up.
- Explain the reduction method well enough to teach it: why centers first, why edge pairing works, why the endgame is just a 3x3.

## Constraints
- Learner already solves a 3x3 comfortably — the 3x3 stage needs no teaching, only the reduction to reach it.
- Physical practice between sessions is available (cube in hand); lessons should end with concrete reps.

## Out of scope
- Speedsolving technique (lookahead, advanced edge-pairing systems like freeslice chains for time, Yau5).
- 4x4 and larger cubes (transfer is a bonus, not a target).
- Blindfolded or one-handed variants.
