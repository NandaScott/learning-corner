Part 2 · The catalog & the cart

What a purchasable thing is, how it enters a cart, and what it costs.

L2 The catalog: products & variantsProduct vs variant vs option vs modifier. Variant options generate SKUs and track inventory; modifiers change fulfillment without touching the SKU. Every product has a base variant. → L3 Line itemsThe frozen join between catalog and pipeline. Four types; name a variant by variant_id or by option_selections when modifiers apply; the row captures name/price/options as a snapshot, not a live link. → L4 PricingPrice is an ordered override stack, not a field: default → sale → variant → price list → customer-group / bulk → modifiers → tax. calculated_price is the read-only result; price lists are variant-level and short-circuit later layers. →

Part 3 · Discounts

The four machines behind one word, then the engine that runs two of them.

L5 The discounts landscapeSale price vs automatic promotion vs coupon — on three axes (catalog/cart, coded/not, which object). Automatic vs coupon is one field. And BigCommerce runs two separate coupon systems. → L6 The promotions rule engineOrdered rules, each an action guarded by a condition. Conditions (cart/and/or/not), actions (cart_value/cart_items/gift_item/shipping/fixed_price_set), BOGO, and how stacking resolves. First match wins. →
